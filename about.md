We started Manila Sun with the simple premise that people want to learn more about Filipino food and culture.

Whether you're a Filipino American discovering their roots, or someone who wants to learn more about Filipino cuisine, you're in the right place.

## What We Cover

Manila Sun explores Filipino food one dish at a time. Each article digs into the history, preparation, cultural significance, and where to find these dishes in the United States. From breakfast staples like [tocino](/tocino/) to celebratory noodles like [pancit](/pancit/), we aim to be a go-to resource for anyone curious about Filipino cuisine.

## Who We Are

Manila Sun was founded by writers and food enthusiasts with roots in the Filipino diaspora. Our contributors include Amanda Natividad and Amber Kanuckel, who bring first-hand cultural knowledge and a love for sharing Filipino traditions with a wider audience.
